Bobby Kohls is a student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison studying Personal Finance with an emphasis in financial planning. He is also working towards certificates in business, entrepreneurship, and digital studies. Bobby is also involved in numerous clubs on campus. He is a member of the Student Retail Association (SRA) and Financial Occupations Club for University Students (FOCUS). He is also seeking a board position as Treasurer in Phi Upsilon Omicron (PhiU), the School of Human Ecology's honor society. Bobby also works as a teller at St. Mary's & Affiliates Credit Union (SMACU) here in Madison. This job is great as SMACU is a smaller firm and has enabled Bobby to gain insights into every niche of the business. After school, Bobby hopes to begin a career in finance and eventually become an investment fund manager.

Outside of school and work, Bobby enjoys spending time with his family and friends. He makes occasional trips back home to his hometown, West Bend, WI, to make appearances at birthday parties or other special occasions. However, his favorite part about coming home is the ability to see his two dogs, Haley (right) and Dakota (left).
